**WolvesVille** is a real-time multiplayer social deduction game where villagers must find the werewolves hiding among them before it's too late!

---

## üéÆ How to Play

1.  **Create a Lobby**: Enter your name and choose an avatar to host a new game.
2.  **Invite Friends**: Share the **Game Code** or the **Link** with your friends. You need at least 4 players to start!
3.  **Roles Assigned**: Each player is secretly assigned a role (Villager, Werewolf, Seer, Doctor, Witch, Avenger, etc.).
4.  **Phases**:
    *   üåô **Night**: Special roles (Werewolves, Seer, Doctor, Witch) perform their actions.
    *   ‚òÄÔ∏è **Day**: Everyone discusses who they think the wolves are.
    *   üó≥Ô∏è **Vote**: Players vote to eliminate a suspect.
5.  **Win**: 
    *   **Villagers Win** by eliminating all Werewolves.
    *   **Werewolves Win** by outnumbering the Villagers.

## üöÄ Strategy Tips

*   **Communication is Key**: Use the chat to share information (or spread misinformation!).
*   **Trust No One**: Even your best friend might be a wolf in disguise.
*   **Use Abilities Wisely**: The Witch only has one of each potion per game. The Seer should be careful not to reveal themselves too early.
